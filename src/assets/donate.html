Sky View Café — Donations

If you enjoy using Sky View Café and would like to express your appreciation for the the web site, you can do so by making a donation. Simply click on the donation button below and donate whatever amount you feel is appropriate.

Just to toot my own horn a bit for a moment... I've found out that a lot of people assume that there's a group or some sort of organization behind Sky View Café. This web site, the Sky View Café web app, and the legacy Java applet with which it all started, are actually all the work of just one guy — me, Kerry Shetline. I've never thought about this web site as a profit center (and I'd have turned out to be a terrible business person if I had!), but since I've poured thousands of hours of effort into developing Sky View Café, I wouldn't mind seeing this labor of love generate at least a little extra money to help defray the costs of hosting the web site on a dedicated web server. I've decided to set up this donations page as my way of passing a virtual hat around the room and seeing if the audience likes what they've found here enough to contribute to that goal.
